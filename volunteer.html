Phipps is known for its beautiful gardens, flower shows, and sustainability initiatives and programs. However, none of these things would be possible without the help of our volunteers. Interested in helping out? There are a multitude of roles that you can take, from running our summer camp to introducing visitors to our sustainability initiatives!

Volunteer Testimonial

Joan Star - Pittsburgh, PA, Summer Camp Counselor

Being a summer camp counselor at Phipps was one of the most rewarding experiences of my life. I worked with children of various ages from June to August, and helped them learn about nature, science, and healthy living. Not only was I contributing to the community, but I learned so many new things as well! My time at Phipps was definitely rewarding!
